Have you tried file upload?

The Friend Workspace is a great cloud storage facility! Have you tried dragging a file from your local PC and dropping it on the Workspace?

To put your files into Friend is easy - just move your browser window to the side, and select files and folders from your local PC or Mac, and drag them into the Friend Workspace. Your files will be uploaded to your Home:Downloads/ folder.

You can also upload files to a specific folder. For example, you can open Home:Documents/ in the Friend Workspace - and then drag your local files and folders into that file window, and the upload will place your files there.
