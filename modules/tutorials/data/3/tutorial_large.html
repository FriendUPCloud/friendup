Have you tried the right click menu?

When using the right mouse button over various objects, you get a helpful menu that allows you to do something with it. We call that the context menu.

Try using your right click menu over icons, the dock or the Workspace wallpaper. The menu items tell what you can do - and they change depending on what you have clicked. Try clicking on an icon, the Workspace wallpaper or the Dock for different options.
