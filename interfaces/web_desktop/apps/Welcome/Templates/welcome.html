Friend Chat

As the central pivot point of collaboration in Friend OS, Friend Chat comes fully featured with real time chat, collaboration features, video conferencing and audio calls. Collaborate in groups or one-to-one. Invite friends and co-workers, and share files or images.

Friend Chat is meant to give you both the efficiency as well as the intimacy of being in an office, or co-working space, with your friends and colleagues. Communication is direct and ad-hoc - like in real life. We hope you will enjoy it.

Video conferencing

Friend Conference is our professional meeting app for hosting larger, pre-planned video conferences. The application has several pro features, like "raise hand", moderation and meeting management. Additionally, it offers cool effects, like "change background", to make the experience more - or even less - personal.

Friend Conference is very simple to use. It also has a professional version, which gives you 2FA authentication features as well as multiple room management options.

Your multitasking office workflow

As a complete office suite for working online, Friend Office gives you all the tools you need for authoring documents, spreadsheets and visual presentations.

With Friend's multitasking Workspace, you may enjoy the efficiency of working on multiple documents at the same time, side-by-side on your screen.

Design flowcharts, edit diagrams

When planning or describing technology architecture, projects or design solutions – or simply making charts for presentations or documentation – DrawIO is a powerful app to consider.

DrawIO is integrated with Friend, and in addition to being able to store and open .drawio files from Friend storage folders - it can autosave, allowing you to concentrate on your work.

Edit photos, design graphical layouts

Photopea gives you most of the features found in applications like PhotoShop, Sketch and The Gimp. It even supports popular image file formats.

Photopea is integrated in Friend OS, and supports Friend storage folders as well as drag & drop. This means that you can manage and work on your projects right inside of the Friend Workspace.
