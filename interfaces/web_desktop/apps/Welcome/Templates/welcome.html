For business

Friendly environment for business

If you have a business, or are part of a company, Friend offers a plethora of possibilities. The Friend Unifying Platform is a multi-user meta operating system, offering a device agnostic computing environment accessible via the Friend Workspace with an HTML5 and Javascript based user interface, where file management and applications can be run.​

Friend is filled with features and functionality for business users. It offers a truly interactive cloud environment where you can mix and match Windows applications, legacy applications and web applications and distribute access to large groups of users across all devices, from a smartphone to a high end workstation.

Integration and automation

Friend offers new and innovative ways to support your business as well as benefiting your customers in the process. Friend allows for seamless integration, automation and unique work flow design giving true meaning to digital transformation.
